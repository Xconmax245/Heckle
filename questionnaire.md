# Heckle - Questionnaire

### 1. What does your application/service do?
Heckle is an AI-powered mobile app launch simulator. It helps developers, founders, and creators pressure-test their launch messaging (such as App Store descriptions, Product Hunt pitches, launch tweet threads, TikTok captions, or investor elevator pitches) against a panel of simulated AI personas (e.g., skeptical early adopters, busy tech journalists, impatient social media scrollers, app store browsers, app investors, etc.) before going live. It generates a detailed Launch Impact scorecard (assessing Download Intent, Shareability, Media Potential, and Store Conversion), detects internal messaging contradictions, recommends alternative launch assets (promotion coverage map), and suggests targeted copy rewrites.

### 2. Who is the target audience?
Our target audience includes:
- **Indie Hackers & Solo Developers** who build products quickly and want to ensure their marketing copy is just as good as their code.
- **Early-Stage Startup Founders & Product Managers** who need to validate their messaging and positioning before presenting to investors or the public.
- **App Marketing Copywriters & Growth Hackers** who want to optimize App Store listings and conversion rates.
- **Student Entrepreneurs** entering startup or app development competitions who want professional-grade feedback.

### 3. Which countries are the expected buyers of this service?
Expected buyers are software developers and startup founders globally, with a strong focus on active tech hubs and app-centric economies:
- **North America:** United States and Canada (high concentration of indie developers, startup activity, and SaaS purchasing power).
- **Europe:** United Kingdom, Germany, France, Netherlands, Sweden, and Ireland (thriving tech hubs and strong digital product adoption).
- **Asia-Pacific:** India, Australia, Singapore, Japan, and South Korea (rapidly growing developer ecosystems, digital transition, and startup initiatives).

### 4. Who are your competitors?
- **General-Purpose AI Writing Tools (e.g., ChatGPT, Copy.ai, Jasper):** While they can write copy, they do not offer multi-persona stakeholder simulation, specialized mobile metrics, contradiction detection, or dedicated launch strategy maps.
- **Community Feedback Forums (e.g., Product Hunt, Indie Hackers, Reddit, Slack/Discord groups):** These provide human feedback, but are slow (taking days/weeks), can have a high noise-to-signal ratio, and carry the risk of public roasting or copycat risk before an official launch.
- **Professional Copywriting Agencies:** Highly skilled, but extremely expensive ($500 to $5000+ per campaign) and slow.

### 5. What is your advantage?
- **Specialized Multi-Persona Panel:** Instead of a single chatbot response, Heckle runs the launch copy through up to 6 independent simulated personas simultaneously, providing multi-perspective feedback in seconds.
- **Actionable Launch Scorecard:** Concrete metrics tailored specifically to mobile app launches (Download Intent, Shareability, Media Potential, and Store Conversion).
- **Contradiction Detector:** A smart analysis phase that scans copy for conflicting claims (e.g., claiming the app is "fully automated" in one line but "hand-curated" in another).
- **Automated Promo Kit:** Instantly generates secondary assets (Product Hunt posts, X threads, Reddit posts, press emails, social captions) adapted from the optimized core pitch.
- **Premium Editorial UX:** Employs a beautiful editorial/newspaper-inspired theme with custom Web Audio micro-interactions instead of a generic chat interface, making launch feedback engaging.
